The Challenge

We face a dual crisis: the social isolation of seniors who hold a lifetime of invaluable industry knowledge, and the "experience gap" faced by youth entering an increasingly competitive workforce.

Traditional mentorship is often inaccessible, expensive, or lacks rigorous verification, leaving vast amounts of human capital untapped.

Trust Infrastructure

BridgeTheGap operates on an evidence-based Trust Infrastructure. Our Verification Vault ensures that every mentor is exactly who they claim to be.

  • Immutable Documents: Certificates are uploaded securely to Amazon S3.
  • AI Verification: Amazon Rekognition cross-references IDs automatically.
  • 1-on-1 Sessions: 20-minute focused mentoring calls via AWS Chime.
